Education in Pittsburgh

Education continues to be a struggle for the SB community and a high school diploma is a significant achievement. Roughly 80% of youth ages 18 - 25 have graduated from high school and approximately 10 youth have gone on to graduate from a trade school or 2- or 4-year college. While there have been success stories among the “next generation,” poverty and illiteracy have taken their toll on the community.

Building Bridges for Education

The volunteer community working group has developed and implemented initial strategies including a communication bridge between teachers and parents and a youth job fair held in Northview Heights. Building Bridges for Education will continue this work, expanding the outreach and providing additional resources.
