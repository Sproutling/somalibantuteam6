University of Pittsburgh, 4200 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15260

An opportunity for volunteers to contribute their time and skills to local projects that directly benefit the Somali Bantu community.

Every Friday at 6:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15213

Cultural Celebration at Pitt

December 9th at 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM

University of Pittsburgh, 4200 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15260
